About Us

Directed by Dr. Mingxiao Yang, the Integrative Health (iHEALTH) Lab at Nanyang Technological University is focused on pioneering integrative medicine research, particularly in oncology, neuroscience of pain, insomnia, and metabolic health.

Research Areas

  • Integrative therapies in chronic health conditions
  • Neuroscience and translational omics research
  • Digital health and AI-driven innovations

Ongoing Projects

  • Evidence Map Study of Traditional Chinese Medicine in Cancer Symptom Care
  • Oncology Massage Therapy: A Systematic Review
