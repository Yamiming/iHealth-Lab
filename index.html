Welcome to the iHEALTH Lab

About Us

Welcome to iHEALTH Lab — a young, dynamic research group dedicated to integrative healthcare. Our lab integrates clinical, translational, and technology-enhanced research with mentorship, teaching, and global partnerships. Led by Dr. Mingxiao Yang, our focus is on evidence-based innovations to improve health worldwide. We welcome students, fellows, and collaborators to join us in this journey.

Lab Focus

We specialize in:

  • Developing integrative therapies (acupuncture, herbal medicine, Tai Chi, exercise) to manage chronic conditions like cancer side effects, metabolic syndrome, and cardiovascular diseases.
  • Exploring the neurobiological and psychosocial mechanisms of sensory function, cognition, and memory via omics, neuroimaging, and sensory testing.
  • Promoting digital and AI-enabled tools for practical integration of evidence-based therapies into healthcare settings.

Projects

From NIH R21 trials to metabolomics of acupuncture, our lab leads and collaborates on multiple global and interdisciplinary projects targeting real-world health solutions. Topics include Tai Chi for CIPN, dopamine metabolism in insomnia, and herbal therapies for cardiovascular and women’s health.
