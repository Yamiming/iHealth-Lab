      <p>Directed by Dr. Mingxiao Yang, the Integrative Health (iHEALTH) Lab at Nanyang Technological University is focused on pioneering integrative medicine research, particularly in oncology, neuroscience of pain, insomnia, and metabolic health. Our lab bridges clinical and translational science through innovative omics technologies and digital healthcare advancements.</p>

    <section id=\"research\">
      <h2>Research Areas</h2>
      <ol>
        <li><strong>Integrative Therapies and Chronic Health Conditions:</strong> Acupuncture, herbal medicine, Tai Chi, and exercise to mitigate side effects from cancer treatments, metabolic syndrome, and cardiovascular diseases.</li>
        <li><strong>Neuroscience and Translational Omics:</strong> Exploring the neurobiological, psychosocial, and behavioral underpinnings of pain, cognition, and memory, leveraging neuroimaging, metabolomics, and quantitative sensory testing.</li>
        <li><strong>Digital Health Innovations:</strong> Promoting evidence-based, accessible integrative healthcare through digitization, artificial intelligence, virtual care models, and implementation science.</li>
      </ol>
